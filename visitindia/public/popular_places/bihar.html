Famous Places in Bihar

Bodh Gaya

A major Buddhist pilgrimage site where Buddha attained enlightenment.

Nalanda

Ancient center of learning and one of the world's first residential universities.

Patna

The state capital known for its historical sites and vibrant culture.

Pawapuri

A sacred Jain pilgrimage site where Lord Mahavira attained nirvana.

Rajgir

An ancient city with historical and religious significance, including Buddhist and Jain sites.

Vaishali

An important historical and archaeological site with Buddhist and Jain ruins.

Katihar

Known for its natural beauty and historical significance.

Madhubani

Famous for its traditional Madhubani art and cultural heritage.

Sasaram

Known for its historical monuments, including the tomb of Sher Shah Suri.

Kushinagar

A significant Buddhist site where Buddha is believed to have passed away.

Food in Bihar

Bihar's cuisine is known for its simplicity and rich flavors. Popular dishes include litti chokha, sattu paratha, and the sweet delicacy of khaja. The food is often enjoyed with a side of traditional pickles and chaas.

Hotels in Bihar

Bihar offers a variety of accommodation options, from budget hotels in Patna to heritage stays in historical areas. Choices range from luxury to economical stays suited for different needs.
