Famous Places in Maharashtra

Maharashtra is home to numerous landmarks and attractions that reflect its rich history and cultural diversity.

Ajanta Caves

An ancient rock-cut cave complex known for its Buddhist murals and sculptures.

Ellora Caves

Famous for its rock-cut temples and monasteries representing Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism.

Elephanta Caves

Known for its large rock-cut sculptures of Hindu gods and goddesses.

Gateway of India

A historic monument in Mumbai built during the British Raj.

Maharashtra Forts

A collection of historical forts such as Raigad, Sinhagad, and Pratapgad.
